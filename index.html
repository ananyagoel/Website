About Us

As the world scales towards the future of air mobility, our AI platform enables an affordable on-demand air transportation for the world. We help air operators provide the convenience of on-demand air service at Uber-like prices with our simultaneous approach towards real-time route planning, optimizing battery usage, revenue management and more. With a team that has over a decade of experience in aerospace engineering, air transportation system, and solving complex optimization problems, we are determined to turn this bold vision to reality.
